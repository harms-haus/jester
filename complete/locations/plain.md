# Plain

## Basic Information
- **Name**: Plain
- **Type**: Biome

## Description
A dry, flat region that enables farming, husbandry, sports that require large flat areas, etc. The grasses are tall, the flowers sway in the wind, the sun is bright above with little cover from trees.
